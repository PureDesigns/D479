Sightseeing

Beaches

Bring your swim wear and don't forget the sunscreen. We have many different beaches to choose from, which includes both sandy and rocky tourane. All have the perfect access to crystal clear water for your enjoyment.

Tours

Don't miss out on our bus tour for a fun adventure around the island where we will take you into the heart of Taniti and show you the most exciting experiences. Or sign up for the boat tour where you will see the beautiful outlay of our island.

Rainforest

Take a stroll through the lush, tropical rainforest where we have cleared paths for easy access. You will see exotic birds, trees, and foliage.

Volcano

Come see the mesmerizing glow of our small active volcano, best viewed at night. Or take a climb on our mountainous tourane. The surrounding area is covered in rock so wear appropriate shoes.
