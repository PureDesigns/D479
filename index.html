Lodgings

Hostels

Needing to save some money on where you stay? Try checking out our hostels where you rent a bunk in our gender seperated rooms. Each dorm hosts a bathroom with shower areas and multiple sinks to cut down on the wait time.

Resorts

If you are interested in a luxury stay at the Island, come check out our resort selection. We have an assortment of rooms, ranging from large furnished suites, to some having access to our private beach area. Call the number to book your room.

Hotels

We have a 5 star hotel that provides bed and breakfast. Each room has its own TV, couch, bathroom, and kitchen area. Second and third story rooms showcase a beautiful view of the surrounding island.

B&Bs

Book a stay at one of our Bed-and-Breakfast locations where we provide everything you need for a wonderful time at Taniti Island. Make our B&B, your B&B where you have a home away from home.
