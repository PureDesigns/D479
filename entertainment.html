Entertainment

Snorkeling

You can either join us on a guided tour of the reef to showcase the wildlife and exotic undersea plants or have fun on your own exploring life under the water. You won't want to miss this!

Zip-Lining

Experience the thrill of flying through the air on our rainforest zip-line.

Helicopter Rides

Schedule a ride in our helicopter where we take you around the island. Each ride is about 15 to 20 minutes long.

Fishing Tour

Charter a boat with a native islander to show you the way they grew up fishing. The tour will go off the coast of the island and will teach how to catch, clean, and cook fish.

Pubs

Taniti Island has several pubs where we serve drinks and food. Entertainment is also provided in an island fashion between 6-8pm. Also consider seeing our microbrewery.

Dance Club

If you feel like dancing the night away, come hang out at our night club where we pump modern music along with some island classics.

Movie Theater

Come catch a movie at our 100 seat theater. Kick back and relax with some popcorn and a large soft drink as you watch our 50 foot cinema screen.

Museum

Our local history museum will give you an insight into how our island got its start.

Art Gallery

Don't count out the art gallery. See the local art of the island along with how it has progressed through the ages.

Arcade

Great for kids and adults alike, our arcade is filled with games everyone will like.

Bowling

Located nextdoor to the arcade is the bowling alley. Schedule a lane or come as you are to hang out with family or friends.
